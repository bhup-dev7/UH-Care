# UH Care - Healthcare Management System

![UH Care Logo](https://img.shields.io/badge/UH%20Care-Healthcare%20Platform-blue)

## Overview

**UH Care** is a comprehensive healthcare management platform designed for **United Home Care**. It provides integrated solutions for managing patient care, appointments, pharmacy services, medical equipment rentals, laboratory requests, and physiotherapy sessions.

## Features

### Core Modules

- **📅 Appointments Management** - Schedule and manage patient appointments with healthcare providers
- **🏠 Care at Home** - Track and manage home-based care services for patients
- **💊 Pharmacy Orders** - Manage medication inventory and patient prescriptions
- **🦽 Equipment Rentals** - Oversee medical equipment rentals and availability
- **🧬 Lab Requests** - Process and track laboratory test requests and reports
- **🏃 Physiotherapy** - Manage physiotherapy sessions and treatment plans
- **👥 Patient Management** - Comprehensive patient records and profile management
- **👨‍⚕️ Staff Management** - Manage healthcare team members and their schedules
- **📊 Reports & Analytics** - Generate insights and reports for business intelligence
- **💬 Messaging** - Internal communication system for staff coordination

### Admin Features

- User and role management
- System configuration and settings
- Activity monitoring and logging
- Real-time notifications and alerts
